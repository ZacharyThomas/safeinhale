What we're doing

 

CleanAirForMe is a website and hardware hack dedicated to giving you notifications on your home CO, CH4, and H20 Humidity levels while you are both home or away.

Too many people die each year from accidental Carbon Monoxide poisoning, too many houses are damaged by humidity, and too many homes are destroyed by gas leaks that go undetected. Enter, CleanAirForMe. We wanted to dedicate our time to providing you with clean air.

If your air is even the slightest bit out of the ordinary, we'll have you informed via webiste and your home filtration system turned on. If that isn't enough, we'll contact the authorities to help you at your home. Your comfort and safety are our greatest priority and hope to have clean air for you at all times!
